## 🔗 Project Basics

*   **Project Title:** NeuroBridge
*   **Elevator Pitch (Under 200 words):**
    > NeuroBridge is an AI-native communication copilot designed to solve the 80% unemployment rate among autistic adults. The primary barrier to their employment is not technical ability, but the social friction, ambiguity, and jargon of neurotypical workplace communication.
    > 
    > Operating directly inside Slack and Email, NeuroBridge uses Gemini to act as a real-time interpreter. It instantly translates vague corporate messages (like "needs some love") into literal, step-by-step checklists, detects hidden subtext, and drafts professional, tone-adjusted replies. Uniquely, it also provides context-aware sensory pacing tips to prevent cognitive overload and burnout.
    > 
    > For businesses, NeuroBridge is a B2B SaaS tool funded by corporate accommodation and DEI budgets, helping employers retain highly focused, elite technical talent. For users, it removes daily communication anxiety. By integrating into existing enterprise suites, NeuroBridge is built to scale rapidly to over 100,000 users, turning neurodiversity into a competitive advantage.
